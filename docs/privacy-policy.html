Uni-Vishwas - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Uni-Vishwas Android app (package name com.univishwas.app, "the app") handles information when you use it. The app is the official mobile client for the Uni-Vishwas residential-society portal hosted at learninganddevelopment.net/Uninav/Application/.

1. Who runs the app

The app is published and operated by the Uni-Vishwas society management ("we", "us"). For any privacy question or data-deletion request, contact us at privacy@uni-vishwas.example. (Replace this address with your actual contact email before publishing the policy URL to the Play Console.)

2. What the app does

The app is a thin WebView wrapper around the Uni-Vishwas web portal. All account creation, login, notices, complaints, polls and classifieds happen on the web portal; the app simply renders those pages and adds native conveniences such as camera-based image uploads, file downloads, pull-to-refresh and persistent login.

3. Information we collect

Category What it is Where it is processed
Account credentials Your username and password, entered into the login form on the web portal inside the WebView. Sent directly to the Uni-Vishwas web server. The app code never reads, stores or transmits your password itself.
Session cookie A standard PHP session cookie (UNINAVSESS) issued by the server after you log in, so you do not have to log in every time you open the app. Stored on your device by the system WebView's CookieManager. Cleared when you log out or clear the app's storage.
Photos you choose to upload Profile pictures, complaint images, notice images and classified-ad images that you explicitly attach inside the app. Uploaded directly to the Uni-Vishwas server over the network. The photos are not sent anywhere else.
Downloaded files Poll-result PDFs and similar documents that you tap to download. Saved to your device's standard Downloads folder by Android's built-in DownloadManager.
Network state Whether the device currently has Internet connectivity, used only to show the offline retry screen. Read in-memory by the app; never stored or transmitted.

The app does not collect or transmit your precise or approximate location, contacts, calendar, microphone audio, SMS messages, call logs, advertising ID, device identifiers, or any analytics or crash-reporting data. The app contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

4. Why the app requests each permission

PermissionWhy
INTERNET To load the Uni-Vishwas web portal inside the WebView.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE To detect when the device is offline and show a retry screen.
CAMERA Lets you capture a photo with the camera only when you tap an upload control inside the app (for example, "change profile photo" or "attach image to complaint"). The camera is never activated automatically or in the background.
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+) / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below) Lets you pick an existing photo from your gallery when you tap an upload control. The app only opens the picker; it does not scan your photo library otherwise.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below only) Lets the system save downloaded poll PDFs to the public Downloads folder on older Android versions where this permission is still required. Modern Android versions handle downloads via scoped storage and do not need this permission.

5. How we share information

Information you submit through the app (uploads, complaints, polls, etc.) is sent to the Uni-Vishwas web server so it can be processed by the society's portal. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with advertisers or unrelated third parties. We may disclose information when legally required to do so by a court order or law enforcement request.

6. Data retention and deletion

Data you create on the web portal (profile, complaints, ad listings, poll responses) is retained for as long as your society membership is active, in line with the portal's data-retention practices. To request deletion of your account or specific content, email privacy@uni-vishwas.example from the address registered with your account, or use the account-deletion option inside the portal where available.

Locally on your device you can clear the app's stored data at any time via Android Settings -> Apps -> Uni-Vishwas -> Storage -> Clear storage, which removes the saved session cookie and the WebView cache. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

7. Children

The app is intended for residents of the Uni-Vishwas society and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Security

The session cookie is stored using Android's standard WebView cookie store, which is sandboxed to this app. The app disables WebView file access (setAllowFileAccess(false)) so web content cannot read arbitrary files on your device. Cleartext HTTP is permitted only for the Uni-Vishwas portal domain; all other domains must use HTTPS. We are migrating the portal to HTTPS and will remove the cleartext exemption in a future version.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how the app handles personal data, we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after the change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
