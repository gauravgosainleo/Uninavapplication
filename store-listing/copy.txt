Full description (max 4000 chars; use plain text, no HTML)
-----------------------------------------------------------
Uni-Vishwas is the official residents' app for your housing society.
Stay on top of every notice, raise and track complaints, vote in polls,
and browse classifieds posted by your neighbours - all from one place.

Built for everyday society life:

* Persistent login - sign in once and the app remembers you.
* Notices and circulars, delivered the moment management posts them.
* Complaints and maintenance requests with photo attachments from your
  gallery or camera, and full status tracking.
* Polls and surveys with one-tap PDF result downloads.
* Classified ads from neighbours - rent, buy, sell, recommend.
* Profile photo upload from camera or gallery.
* Pull-to-refresh on every screen so you always see the latest.
* Hardware Back navigation that follows the app's own history.
* Material 3 light and dark themes that match your system setting.
* Offline screen with a one-tap retry button when the network drops.

Permissions, why we ask:

* Camera - only when you tap an upload control to capture a photo for
  your profile, a complaint, or a classified. The camera is never
  activated automatically or in the background.
* Photos & media - to let you pick existing pictures from your gallery
  for the same upload features. We do not scan or upload your library.
* Internet & network state - to load the society portal and to show an
  offline retry screen when the connection drops.

Privacy:
We do not include any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. The
app stores only your standard web session cookie on the device, and
content you submit (uploads, complaints, polls) goes directly to the
society's web server. The full privacy policy is linked from the
listing.

Uni-Vishwas works on Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and newer.
